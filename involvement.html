Join Us

If you share our belief that health, prosperity and fun are grounded in our access to the earth, and our care of it, then join us. You can make a difference by being an active and valued member of the Mariposa Trails community. Volunteers are the heart of the Mariposa Trails organization, shaping its vision, enlivening its mission, and achieving its goal to maintain and promote recreational trail access for hikers, runners, cyclists, equestrians and others. Many volunteer options exist, including:

  • Trail Day Events
  • Recreation Day Events
  • Race Events
  • Youth Outings
  • Trail and Ales Events
  • Leadership & Support Positions

Leadership and Support

Mariposa Trails implements several programs and initiatives to build trails and community, enabling a wide range of tasks for volunteers to fill, including: grant writers, educators, photographers, graphic artists, chefs, financiers, carpenters, scientists, event planners and coordinators, recreation trip leaders and partner ambassadors. Together, we can achieve the vision of Mariposa Trails to create spaces and activities that benefit the health of Mariposans and our guests, the prosperity of our community, and our relationship with nature. Choose to "pay it forward," knowing that our health, prosperity and enjoyment of our lands are rooted in our access to the earth, and our care of it.

Safety

The safety of people is the primary consideration in all of Mariposa Trails activities. Without exception, no activity is to be attempted by Mariposa Trails leaders, members, volunteers or associates unless it can be done safely. We work to achieve trail safety by identifying and repairing hazards whenever possible and encouraging the use of common sense and caution by all trail users. To this end:

  • All trail-related events start with a safety talk
  • Safe and operational tools, helmets, gloves and goggles are provided
  • Educators
  • First-Aid Medical kits on every outing
  • Chainsaw operation certification is required
  • Training events are offered
