Volunteer

Volunteers are the heart of the Mariposa Trails Community, enlivening its mission and realizing its goals. While building trails may be the most outward expression of Mariposa Trails, it’s building community that we strive to be our legacy. Given our broad platform of goals, there are many roles for volunteers, including: writers, educators, artists, chefs, carpenters, planners, scientists and event coordinators. If you share our belief that health, prosperity and fun are grounded in our access to the earth, and our care of it, then join us. You can make a difference by being an active and valued member of the Mariposa Trails team.



Join Us

As a non-profit trail centric organization we rely on people volunteering to help us meet our goals and objectives. In addition to the need for people to volunteer for trail maintenance, there is a wide array of roles volunteers can fill, including:

  • Grant Writing
  • News Editor/Writing
  • Educators
  • Food Prep and Delivery
  • Membership Development
  • Finance and Fundraising
  • Graphic Design
  • Photography
  • Trail Advocates and Planners


Safety

The safety of people is the primary consideration in all of Mariposa Trails activities. Without exception, no activity is to be attempted by Mariposa Trails leaders, members, volunteers or associates unless it can be done safely. We work to achieve trail safety by identifying and repairing hazards whenever possible and encouraging the use of common sense and caution by all trail users. To this end:

  • All trail-related events start with a safety talk
  • Safe and operational tools, helmets, gloves and goggles are provided
  • Educators
  • First-Aid Medical kits on every outing
  • Chainsaw operation certification is required
  • Training events are offered
