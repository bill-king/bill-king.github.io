Join Us

If you share our belief that health, prosperity and fun are grounded in our access to the earth, and our care of it, then join us. You can make a difference by being an active and valued member of the Mariposa Trails community. Volunteers are the heart of the Mariposa Trails organization, shaping its vision, enlivening its mission, and achieving its goal to maintain and promote recreational trail access for hikers, runners, cyclists, equestrians and others. Many volunteer options exist, including:

  • Trail Day Events
  • Recreation Day Events
  • Race Events
  • Youth Outings
  • Trail and Ales Events
  • Leadership & Support Positions

Recreation Events

Discover new places and make new friends while recreating on local Mariposa trails. The Mariposa front-country is an awesome place to hike, bike, run or to simply saunter while enjoying the local wildlife, plants and beautiful scenery. Be a participant, lead a trip on one of your favorite trails, or manage this program.

Race Events

Trail racing, whether on bike or foot, can be a fun way to experience the front-country of Mariposa County. Be a participant, a race event volunteer, or help manage this program. Volunteering on race day is a great way to build friendships and community while earning race apparel and free food and beverages!

Leadership and Support

Mariposa Trails implements several programs and initiatives to build trails and community, enabling a wide range of tasks for volunteers to fill, including: grant writers, educators, photographers, graphic artists, chefs, financiers, carpenters, scientists, event planners and coordinators, recreation trip leaders and partner ambassadors. Together, we can achieve the vision of Mariposa Trails. Choose to "pay it forward," knowing that our health, prosperity and enjoyment of our lands are rooted in our access to the earth, and our care of it.
