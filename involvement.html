Membership

You can make a difference by being an active and valued member of the MARIPOSA TRAILS community. Volunteers are the heart of the MARIPOSA TRAILS organization, shaping its vision, enlivening its mission, and achieving its goal to maintain and promote recreational trail access and use. Volunteers are needed to help operate our programs, including:

  • Trail-Tending Outings
  • Recreational Trips
  • Race Events
  • Youth Pathways
  • Trail and Ales Gatherings

BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP

  • no fee
  • Our bi-annual e-newsletter
  • Invitations to our special events
  • Opportunities to be part of stewardship and advocacy efforts

Together, we can achieve the vision of MARIPOSA TRAILS. Choose to "pay it forward," knowing that our health, prosperity and enjoyment of our lands are rooted in our access to the earth, and our care of it.

Trail Tending Outings

Give back to the lands that nourish your spirit. Trail day events are much more than moving dirt and boulders; spend time with friends enjoying backcountry scenic views, wildlife and wildflowers, while creating a space for others to do the same. Review the list of events below and RSVP soon and often! NOTE: If an event is full, then it won't appear in the sign-up list. Anticipating lessening of "stay-at-home" status here in Mariposa County, we are scheduling trail-tending events should that occur. Sign-up and stay tuned.

COVID-19 RELATED SAFETY PROTOCOLS

  • Must be in good health to attend;
  • No carpooling from town to staging area; all must drive separately;
  • No more than 6 people on a trail-tending outing;
  • While hiking single-file, maintain a distance of at least 20-feet;
  • Form maximum sized two-person teams, teams separated from each other by at least 100-feet;
  • While working in teams, maintain a physical distance of 15-feet or more. Assignment of a tool and PPE for each person- no sharing.
  • No large group breaks and lunch. Teams will eat and rest in place. No sharing of food or water;
  • No gatherings immediately preceding or following the event/ sorry - no post outing refreshments

Youth Pathways

MARIPOSA TRAILS embraces youth participation in our programs. This has taken many forms in the past; over time, this is anticipated to expand. To date, MARIPOSA TRAILS have programmed multiday backpacking trail-tending outings with colleges throughout the state; partnered with the US Forest Service for local students to develop cultural interpretation products for use on local trails; and, engaged Mariposa County High School students to manufacture trail wayfinding signs to be placed in recently burned forests.
