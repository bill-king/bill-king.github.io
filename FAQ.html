Tool Use

This fund pays for overhead costs, including, insurance, website, and a modest staff stipend. Staff performs essential day-to-day functions of Mariposa Trails, including organizing and leading trail work events; managing race and recreation events; obtaining and managing grants; coordinating with partners; conducting public outreach; promoting Mariposa Trails; and, securing fiscal sponsorships. Funds collected that exceed the budgeted amounts in this fund are evenly poured into the other funds.


Video

Volunteers bring priceless value to our community. They offer their time and energy working on trails, at race and recreation events, as well as general support. Mariposa trails is grateful and works to reward volunteers with vouchers for food and gas on workdays, by supplying delicious refreshments at the end of trail work days, assuring their tools are sharp and safe to use, and, by hosting an annual volunteer appreciation party. Your donation can help us thank our community of volunteers.
