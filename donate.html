Donations

Mariposa Trails is intentional about maintaining low overhead, is steadfast in our support and value of volunteers, and, maximizes our productivity through partnerships and grantors. In spite of this, financial support from donors is essential to sustaining our work in the community. (Click here to see the 2020 budget). Mariposa Trails offers various funding options that allow donors to specify how their dollars spent. You chose which funds you’d like to contribute to. Choose to pay annually or just one time. No matter how you slice it, we are thankful and respectful of your choice as your financial contribution is a win for you, Mariposa Trails and the community.

Fundraising Options:

  • Sustaining Membership Fund
  • Volunteer Rewards Fund
  • Adopt-a Trail Fund
  • Matching Dollars for Grant Funds
  • Promotion Fund
  • General Fund

Sustaining Membership Fund

This fund pays for overhead costs, including, insurance, website, and a modest staff stipend. Staff performs essential day-to-day functions of Mariposa Trails, including organizing and leading trail work events; managing race and recreation events; obtaining and managing grants; coordinating with partners; conducting public outreach; promoting Mariposa Trails; and, securing fiscal sponsorships. Funds collected that exceed the budgeted amounts in this fund are evenly poured into the other funds.

Membership Levels

  • Trail Guardian ($500+)
  • Trail Builder ($100 –$499)
  • Trail Explorer ($50 –$99) *Cost to seniors and students is $30

Trail Guardians will receive the following benefits: (1) A Mariposa Trail Crew mug and Supporters T-shirt; (2) our Semi Annual Newsletter; and, (3) invitation to our Year End Supporters Party.

Trail Builders receive the following benefits: (1) A Mariposa Trail Crew mug; and, (2) our Semi Annual Newsletter.

Trail Explorers will recieve the Semi Annual Newsletter.

Volunteer Rewards Fund

Volunteers bring priceless value to our community. They offer their time and energy working on trails, at race and recreation events, as well as general support. Mariposa trails is grateful and works to reward volunteers with vouchers for food and gas on workdays, by supplying delicious refreshments at the end of trail work days, assuring their tools are sharp and safe to use, and, by hosting an annual volunteer appreciation party. Your donation can help us thank our community of volunteers.

Adopt-a-Trail Fund

Through our various partnerships, we are authorized to maintain and rebuild many trails in Mariposa, for example the renowned Hite’s Cove Wildflower Trail. While building and maintaining trails is fun and rewarding in itself, Mariposa Trails takes the extra step to promote use of the trails, namely through recreation, races and education. Helping to fund trail work and use of one of your favorite trail is a great reason and great way to support our work and the volunteers that make it happen. Matching Dollars for Grants Fund

Since 2014, Mariposa Trails has contributed thousands of hours of volunteer time to rebuild public trails that would not have been maintained without us. Essential to this work was the award of a grant to purchase trail tools and personnel protective equipment. Most grants, however, require a fiscal contribution from applicants. Absent a ready supply of grant-match funds, the work our volunteers performed would not have happened. Help us be competitive and ready to obtain the funds we need to create trails and activities for our community.

Promotion Fund

This fund is used to promote the Mariposa Trails organization; a donation here is a great way to multiply the dollars we receive from other funding options. Funds are used for shirts, mugs, hats, trail advertising signs, and community tabling events.
