Donations

Mariposa Trails works with our local partners to provide trail planning, maintenance expertise, project grant funding, and volunteer recruitment to augment lack of public funding for trails. Since 2014, Mariposa trails has contributed thousands of hours of volunteer work and helped rebuild public trails that would not have been maintained without us. Without equipment, match funds for grants, and admin support, however, the work our volunteers perform would not happen. Memberships

Membership is free. Recognizing the value that volunteers bring to Mariposa Trails, we are committed to offering events at no cost to them; volunteers should not have to pay to participate. Plus, Mariposa Trails actively seeks ways to support volunteer efforts by seeking food and gas vouchers, and by supplying delicious refreshments at the end of all trail work days.
