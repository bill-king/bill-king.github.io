Memberships

Recognizing the value that volunteers bring to Mariposa Trails, we are committed to offering events at no cost to them; volunteers should not have to pay to participate. Plus, Mariposa Trails actively seeks ways to support volunteer efforts by seeking food and gas vouchers, and by supplying delicious refreshments at the end of all trail work days.
