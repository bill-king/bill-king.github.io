Donations

MARIPOSA TRAILS is intentional about maintaining low overhead, is steadfast in our support and value of volunteers, and, maximizes our productivity through partnerships and grantors. In spite of this, financial support is essential to sustain our work in the community.

MARIPOSA TRAILS offers various fund options that allow donors to specify how their dollars spent. You chose which fund or funds you’d like to contribute to. You could also specify a favorite trail that you’d like to be cared for. No matter how you slice it, we are thankful and respectful of your choice as your financial contribution is a win for you, MARIPOSA TRAILS and the community.


Sustaining Fund

This fund pays for overhead costs, promotional materials and essential day-to-day functions of MARIPOSA TRAILS, including organizing and leading “Tending the Trail” work outings; managing race and recreation events; obtaining and managing grants; coordinating with partners; conducting public outreach; promoting MARIPOSA TRAILS; and, securing fiscal sponsorships.

Volunteer Rewards Fund

To show our appreciation for the time and energy that trail volunteers provide our community, MARIPOSA TRAILS rewards volunteers by covering many costs to host trail work days, including delicious refreshments at the end of trail work days, trail tools and personal protective equipment. We also throw an annual volunteer appreciation party at the end of the season. Help us thank our volunteers by making a donation to this fund.

Matching Grants Fund

Since 2014, MARIPOSA TRAILS has contributed thousands of hours of volunteer time to rebuild community trails. This would not have been possible, however, without grant funds that were used to purchase trail tools and personnel protective equipment. Most grants require a substantive fiscal contribution or match from applicants, however. Donate dollars to this fund to enable us to be able to obtain grant funds we need to create trails and activities for our community.
