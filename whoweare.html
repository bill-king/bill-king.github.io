Who We Are


Mission Statement

Our mission is to create access to public lands and be stewards thereof while promoting their use for personal health and community prosperity.



Our Trail Community

MARIPOSA TRAILS is an unincorporated non-profit organization made up of volunteers, trail advocates and an association of local partnerships. The Upper Mercd River Watershed Council is our fiscal sponsor. As our organization grows, its organizational structure will evolve. Efforts are led by founder Bill King, supported by many volunteers, and guided by an advisory council made up of by the following individuals:

Partners
