Who We Are


Mission Statement

We provide trail access to public lands and promote their use for personal health and community prosperity.



Our Trail Community

MARIPOSA TRAILS is an unincorporated non-profit organization made up of volunteers, trail advocates and an association of local partnerships. The Upper Mercd River Watershed Council is our fiscal sponsor. As our organization grows, its organizational structure will evolve. Efforts are led by founder Bill King, supported by many volunteers, and guided by an advisory council made up of by the following individuals:

Partners

Mariposa Trails believes that planning, building, maintaining and enjoying local trails takes community effort and works with non-profit partners, community organizations, government agencies and special districts to accomplish its vision, mission and goals.
