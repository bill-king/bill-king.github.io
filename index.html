Trails Lead us Together

The mission of MT, in partnership with public and private entities, is to create and maintain a lasting trail network in Mariposa County for the benefit of its lands, people and local economy.

Devils Gulch

Guiding Principles

Mariposa Trails helps bring the community together by emphasizing a few main points:

  • Community Outreach: Mariposa Trails brings people from all backgrounds to come together and maintain our natural surroundings
  • Nature Education: Learning through first hand experience the importance of stewardship
  • Active Lifestyles: Embracing a healthy and active lifestyle

About Us

MT is an unincorporated non-profit, but working toward 501(3)c status, that consists of officers and advisory committees, which follows guiding principles to accomplish objectives in five goal areas:

  • Trail Maintenance
  • TFitness & Community Outreach
  • TLocal Economy
  • Education
  • Trail Advocacy and Planning
