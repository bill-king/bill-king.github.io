OUR PHILOSOPHY

We create and maintain a lasting trail network in Mariposa County through initiatives and programs that create space and activities that benefit the health of the individual, the prosperity of the community and our relationship with nature.

What We Do

We maintain and promote recreational trail access for hikers, runners, cyclists, equestrians and others by tending the trails, promoting fitness & community, expanding the local economy, teaching youth, and advocating for trails.
