About Me

I'm a UCLA graduate with studies in geography, ecology, anthropology, and wilderness management. My work history as an urban planner and current projects weave in these interests, where I connect people with nature, and nature to communities. I do this through several venues: as the founder and operator of the "South Fork Merced River Trailblazers"; as founder and program director at "Got OUTDOORS," as a naturalist guide at "Yosemite Conservancy," and as an urban planner at "Bill King Consulting." I hope to see YOU Somewhere in Yosemite!
